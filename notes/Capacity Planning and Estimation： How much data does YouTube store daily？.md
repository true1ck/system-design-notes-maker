 **Estimation Questions: A Tool for Design Interviews**
=====================================================

In design interviews, estimation questions are a powerful tool to understand users' requirements better. They help designers gain insights into the amount of data or resources needed by their product and can even reveal potential bottlenecks in system performance. This video covers how to approach these types of questions with care while keeping assumptions reasonable.

**Topics Covered:**
1. **Understanding Estimation Questions**: How they work, why we ask them, and the importance of providing clear answers.
2. **Approaching an Estimate Question**: Breaking down complex problems into smaller components to make estimates more manageable.
3. **Avoiding Assumptions in Estimates:** Recognizing common pitfalls when making assumptions about data size, performance requirements, and system capacity.
4. **Tools for Solution Designers**: How estimation questions can help identify potential solutions to problems before they become critical issues. </s>
